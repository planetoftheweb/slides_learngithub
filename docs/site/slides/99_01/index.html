The bootstrap site are extremely well put together and have a lot of examples on how to use the framework. There's actually a tab you should click called examples to take a peek at sample components.

One of the cool pages is called the Cheatsheet, which is a list of samples of all of the components in a convenient place with links to the documentation.

There's also a site that supports bootstrap development where you can purchase Themes that are based on bootsrap.
